## Global Game Jam 簡介

Global Game Jam (GGJ) 是全球性的遊戲開發盛會，吸引了來自世界各地的遊戲開發者參與。在為期48至72小時的活動中，參與者將分組合作，從零開始創作出一款遊戲。每年GGJ都會設定一個主題，激發參賽者的創造力和合作精神。這是一個展現技術、創意與團隊合作能力的絕佳機會。

## 2022年主題：Duality

2022年的Global Game Jam設定了「Duality」（二元性）作為主題。我們的團隊根據這個主題，開發了一款名為「Twilight FoREST」的遊戲。

## 遊戲介紹

「Twilight FoREST」是一款以光與暗的二元性為核心的遊戲。故事講述一位哥哥為了尋找蘋果進入森林，期間必須避免被幽靈追捕。遊戲透過其獨特的視覺風格和挑戰性的遊戲機制，將主題「光與暗」巧妙融入。

## 遊玩方式
- 使用 **WASD** 鍵移動
- 按住 **Shift** 以消耗耐力跑步
- 使用 **滑鼠點擊** 按鈕

## 遊玩規則
- 躲避幽靈追趕。
- 場景中有真蘋果和假蘋果，碰到假蘋果主角會停住不動。
- **幽靈的燈光**會照出真假蘋果的真相。
- 必須撿到符合右上角需求的蘋果數量才能前往房子，並進入下一關。

## 開發過程

在 72 小時的開發期間，團隊成員通過 Discord 伺服器進行溝通，共享討論時的圖片和文件等資訊。我們從彼此陌生開始，經歷了無間斷的合作與努力，最終成功發布了「Twilight FoREST」。

# 2022_GGJ_Twilight-Forest
## Twilight FoREST - Development Record

## About Global Game Jam

Global Game Jam (GGJ) is the world's largest game creation event taking place around the globe. Within a 48 to 72-hour timeframe, participants form teams to create a game from scratch based on a specific theme. It is an excellent opportunity to showcase technical skills, creativity, and teamwork.

## 2022 Theme: Duality

The theme for Global Game Jam 2022 was **"Duality."** Based on this concept, our team developed a game titled **"Twilight FoREST."**

## Game Introduction

"Twilight FoREST" is a game centered around the duality of light and darkness. The story follows a brother who enters a forest in search of apples while avoiding being hunted by ghosts. The game integrates the "Light vs. Dark" theme through its unique visual style and challenging mechanics.

## How to Play
- **WASD**: Move character
- **Shift**: Sprint (consumes stamina)
- **Mouse Click**: Interact with buttons

## Rules
- Evade the pursuing ghosts.
- There are **real** and **fake** apples; hitting a fake apple will freeze the character momentarily.
- The **ghost's light** reveals the true nature of the apples.
- Collect the required number of apples (shown in the top-right corner) to reach the house and proceed to the next level.

## Development Process

During the 72-hour development period, our team collaborated via Discord, sharing sketches, documents, and ideas. Starting as strangers, we worked tirelessly together to successfully launch "Twilight FoREST."
